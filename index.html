Scratch HW

Scratch Summer Homework

Over the summer, I made a game for my AP CS class. In the game, you control the Scratch Cat with your mouse, as you attempt to dodge the bus. This was my favorite thing I made for my assigned Summer HW. I had real trouble deciding on the exact time in which the bus moves, but I worked through it to produce what I think is a pretty fun game.

Favicon Project
